About Us

Welcome to Abyssinian ቅኝት. We Abyssinians offer a great music streaming service that includes Ethiopian songs from the 1970s to early millennium. The website has separate sections for musics by genre, generation, and even by artist album. This makes it easy for you to find what you're looking for.

In addition to streaming musics and categorizing, We also put chronological history of Ethiopian music. This will help you to gain better knowledge and to better acknowledge the musicians who were participated in each work of art but not able to be well recognized. Some radio interviews and documentaries are included below. The videos covers the introduction to the history of Ethiopian music history and a startup histories of some artists.
